Day Planner

Welcome to the 'Day Planner App'!
Type what activites you want to add to your day planner in the box below
and press the 'Add Activity' button (or 'enter' key) to add your activity to your day planner.

If you want to remove an activity added, just simply click on the activity itself or click the cross beside the activity.

You can also set start times for each activity. Click the clock symbol and insert start time.
