Day Planner

Welcome to the 'Day Planner App'. Here you can plan your day ahead.
To get started click 'Start Planning!'.

Start Planning!

  1. Adding activity:
    To add activity, simply type in the activity (where prompted) and click 'Add Activity' button or press 'enter'.
  2. Setting Time:
    To set the starting time of your activity, click on the clock symbol and select desired time, or type in desired time.
  3. Removing activity:
    To remove activity, click on the 'x' beside the desired activity.
